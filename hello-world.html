Hello World

A program to greet the planet.

This pagee is an attempt to greet everyone on Earth using our various human langauges. We're interested to see how long our list can become.

We'll start with languages that the Epicodus staff could offer without using Goolle.

  • English: Hello, World!
  • Spanish: Hola, mundo!
  • Japanese: Konnichiwa, sekai!
  • French: Bonjure, monde!
  • Kinyarwanda: Mwirwe, isi!
  • German: Guten tag, Welt!
  • Pig Latin: ello-hay, orld-way!
